Menemen Recipe

Description

This recipe tells you how to make a delicios traditional Turkish meal, menemen. You will need:

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Seasonings

As for the actual meal, follow these steps:

  1. Butter the pan and heat it for 10 minutes
  2. Add the eggs to the pan
  3. Add tomatoes to the pan
  4. Season as your heart desired
  5. Bon apetit!
