Pizza

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a delicios homemade margarita pizza with everyday ingedients, which are:

  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Water
  • Chease
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Yeast

Once you have the ingredients, now we can actually make the pizza

  1. Put 6 cups of flour into a container
  2. Add 2 cups of water and a tablespoon of olive oil
  3. Add a tablespoon of yeast
  4. Kneed the dough after waiting for about 5 minutes
  5. Once the dough is ready let is rest for 3 hourse
  6. Turn the dough into a pizza shape
  7. Make the sauce by mixing some tamato sauce, salt and pesto
  8. Add sauce to pizza and liberaly add chease
  9. Cook for about 15 minutes in your oven at max temperature
  10. Bon apetit!
